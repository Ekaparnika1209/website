How to control anger effectively ?



Anger is a good thing as long as it does not reach to a boil but is this what is troubling you? Are you not able to control your anger effectively ? No worries! We have a solution for it. Here we have mentioned some measures which would help you manage and control your anger in an effective manner.



01
Stop talking when you feel angry

02
Try to have gratitude

03
Go outside and take a break

04
Find the cues which promote anger and then work on them

05
Practise meditation or yoga

06
Start writing when you are angry
